# 00 Product Brief

## Product

`WallPack AI` is a web app for Etsy sellers who sell printable digital wall art. It turns a seller's concept into a full Etsy-ready digital product pack.

## Target users

### Primary user: Etsy digital wall-art seller

- Sells printable art downloads on Etsy.
- Understands Etsy basics but may not understand DPI, pixel math, or print ratios.
- Wants faster production of cohesive listings.
- Cares about file compatibility and buyer clarity.
- Wants mockups, listing text, and tags alongside the artwork.

### Secondary user: beginner Etsy seller

- Has little design/print knowledge.
- Needs guardrails, templates, and simple explanations.
- Likely starts with low monthly budget.

### Future user: small Etsy shop operator

- Creates many packs in a niche, such as nursery decor, boho prints, botanical art, abstract prints, seasonal decor, Islamic geometry, quote-free minimalism, or farmhouse art.
- Needs batch generation, shop style memory, and bulk listing assets.

## Pain points

1. Generic AI image tools create images, not Etsy products.
2. Sellers do not know which ratios and pixels are needed.
3. Sellers waste time making the same artwork fit 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, 5:7, 11:14, and ISO A sizes.
4. Large print files can exceed Etsy's digital file limits.
5. Mockups and listing copy require extra tools.
6. AI-generated art can accidentally include text, logos, distorted details, or IP-sensitive content.
7. Sellers need an AI-use disclosure line but do not want to write policy-safe copy every time.

## Value proposition

> WallPack AI creates print-ready Etsy wall-art packs, not just AI images.

A pack includes:

- 5 print ratio files.
- Exact 300 DPI pixel dimensions for common print sizes.
- Compressed Etsy-compatible files.
- A buyer instruction PDF.
- Listing title, tags, description, and AI disclosure helper.
- Listing mockups sized for Etsy photos.

## Differentiation vs Canva/ChatGPT

Canva and ChatGPT can generate images, but they do not force a complete Etsy seller workflow. WallPack AI should win through:

- Dedicated print math.
- Etsy file-size constraints.
- Ratio packs.
- Listing asset generation.
- Etsy policy-aware AI disclosure.
- Seller presets.
- Batch workflow.
- Credit/cost predictability.

## Positioning statement

For Etsy sellers who sell printable wall art, WallPack AI is a print-pack generator that converts a creative idea into Etsy-ready files and listing assets. Unlike generic image generators, WallPack AI exports exact print ratios, print sizes, buyer instructions, mockups, and AI-aware listing copy.

## MVP success metrics

- Activation: user exports first pack within first session.
- Time to first pack: under 10 minutes from signup.
- Preview-to-export conversion: at least 25% during beta.
- Failed export rate: under 3%.
- Credit refund due to provider failure: under 5%.
- Percentage of exports under Etsy file limits: over 95%.
- Re-export rate: tracked; high re-export may mean crop/quality issues.

## Product principles

1. Seller outcome over image novelty.
2. Print correctness over fake quality claims.
3. Guided creation over blank prompt boxes.
4. Conservative IP/policy guardrails.
5. Transparent cost and credits.
6. Every output should be directly useful on Etsy.
